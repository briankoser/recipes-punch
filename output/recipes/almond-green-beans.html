Almond Green Beans

By , 2014-07-05T03:10:51.107Z Yield: Serves 6 people Ingredients:
  • 1/4 c. butter, cubed for faster melting
  • 1/4 tsp. chicken bouillon granules
  • 1/4 tsp. celery seed
  • 1/8 tsp. pepper
  • 1 1/4 lbs. fresh or frozen green beans, trimmed
  • 1 1/4 tsp. soy sauce
  • 1/2 c. sliced almonds, toasted
  • salt to taste
Instructions:
  1. Melt butter in large skillet; add bouillon, celery seed and pepper. Stir in the beans; cover and cook over medium heat for 9-11 minutes or until crisp-tender. Stir occasionally.
  2. Stir in soy sauce and almonds; sprinkle with salt and serve.
